📌About Me📝

Hi! I'm Pio Dominic M. Festejo of B'29 Potassium and I'm really into Lego! Formula 1 (and the Lego version of it of course) also stands as one of my interests, with my favorite teams being Mercedes-AMG, Scuderia Ferrari, and Red Bull Racing. In terms of music my personal favorite band is Nirvana. Songs like "Come as You Are" & "The Man Who Sold the World" really catch my attention. Additionally, I do enjoy watching movies, with my favorite one being "Waterloo" (1970). A historical film, shot in the Soviet Union, depicting the Battle of Waterloo & the events leading up to it.

Yet another aspect of mine I'd like to mention is my personality type, ENTP-A. This label entails that I'm the type of person who enjoys engaging in debates and discussions, often playing devil's advocate to challenge theories & opinions. But other than that, I do like conversing with others in a more light or casual manner as well. In other words, I'm actually quite approachable!
